// Capabilities

Run your whole field operation from one system.

FieldPro replaces the spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and paper job sheets you're juggling today — and gives every role a single, live source of truth.

End-to-end job lifecycle

Create, dispatch, accept, service, sign off, and invoice — every job tracked from request to payment with a full audit trail.

Real-time dashboards & KPIs

Live pipeline, completion rates, technician workload, and revenue — leadership sees the entire operation at a glance and acts instantly.

Fleet & hour-meter tracking

Every machine's full history and hour-meter readings, with predictive service scheduling so maintenance never slips through the cracks.

Parts & van-stock inventory

Track every part across the warehouse and each technician's van, with low-stock alerts, movement audit trails, and bulk import.

Billing & invoicing

Turn finished jobs into invoices and quotations in one click, with accounting export — close the gap between work done and cash in.

Mobile technician app

Technicians see their jobs, log parts, capture before/after photos, and collect customer signatures — all from a phone, in the field.

Role-based access

Tailored workspaces for Admin, Supervisor, Technician, and Accountant — everyone sees exactly what they need, nothing they don't.

Customers, sites & equipment

Every customer, site, contact, and machine organized and linked to its complete service history — find anything in seconds.
